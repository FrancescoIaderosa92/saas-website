Our History

This company was founded in 1975 when the owner discovered that there was an easier way to hold annual meetings without having to fly half way across the world to just to make sure enough is was sound with his business.

Shortly after that he sold his shares of his current company and used the funds he received to start this company. We have been going strong for almost 50 years now and if you trust in this comany, we will show you why we are number one in the industry til this day.
